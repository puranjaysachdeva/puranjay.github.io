Rules

  1. Cricket is played between two teams consisting of 11 players each
  2. Each team has an appointed a captain
  3. In cricket captain toss the coin to decide which team is batting or fielding first
  4. There are three umpires in every international cricket game.
  5. There are many pitches on the cricket stadium. Before every match the pitch is been prepared
  6. The groundsman prepare the pitch 1-2 hours before the match begins
  7. Thee is a boundary rope that surrounds the ground.

Ways to score runs

  1. Batsman can score runs by running between the wickets
  2. When the bastman hits the ball along the ground and the ball touches the rope then it is four runs.
  3. When the batsman hits the ball in the air and it bounces inside the boundary rope it is considered as a four
  4. When the bastman hits the ball in the air and the ball hits the rope on the full or goes over it is six runs
